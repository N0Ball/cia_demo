Antarctica

Antarctica. We, Antarcticans, like to call it the Ice.

Don’t get me wrong. Officially human hasn’t set foot on the Ice for over 35 million years until recently. But living there for 54 weeks, having experienced polar day and night on a harsh continent with limited resources, I’m pretty much Antarctican. At least, one in the modern era.
